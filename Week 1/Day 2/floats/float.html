Praying Cats

Cats have been praying for centuries now. Nearly 300 years ago, the first cat, "Kitty McPrayer," prayed her first prayer. What did she pray for? wet food

It is no surprise cats are still praying to this day. Some cats are even believed to pray for non-material things. An after life? Never ending happiness?
